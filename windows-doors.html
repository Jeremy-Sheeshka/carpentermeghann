  • Interior Doors & Entryways

    Sliding doors are sleek, space-saving, and incredibly functional. What’s more, they add a touch of drama by creating an engaging transition between spaces.

    With the right wood and finish, sliding doors can cozy up to any style, from modern horizontal lines to distinctive rustic farmhouse.

    I relish the challenge of creating these pieces that not only move with grace but also stand as a testament to functional craftsmanship.

  • Exterior Awnings & Arbors

    A well-crafted swinging gate or door isn’t just an inanimate creation; it’s essentially alive.

    The movement is what brings life to the object itself, while also bringing a whole new level of craftsmanship to the project.

    Swinging doors and gates have to flex, sway, swing, and be “worked”. I love building objects that have movement, where the beauty lies in the subtleties as much as the materials and design.

FAQs

Here are some of the questions I am most commonly asked about building doors and gates.

View all FAQs

Do you design and build custom wooden gates?

Yes I do! I can build your gate to any specific style or size that meets your preferences and needs.

Do you install store-bought or prefab gates?

Yes, I am happy to install a prefabricated or store-bought gate within your project.

What materials can be used to build custom doors?

Custom doors can be made from a wide variety of materials. Of course I have a fondness for working with wood, but can also incorporate metal, glass, and even recycled materials. The choice of materials depends on your preferences, budget, and the desired look and functionality of the doors.

Can you replace my interior and/or exterior doors?

Yes I can. Depending on the condition of the frame, I can either replace just the slab or install a new pre-hung unit.
