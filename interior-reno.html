FAQs

Here are some of the questions I am most commonly asked about windows and doors.

I have crack in the walls of my house! Can you help me fix it?

Yes! Cracks in drywall may be signs of how the house's foundation or joists have settled over time and most often can be fixed through remediatory drywall work.

I want to remove my old dusty carpet and install vinyl plank flooring, can you do this for me?

Yes ofcourse! I have experience in laying down a different types of flooring and can make sure the subfloor is properly prepped to ensure a squak-free and level installation.

I want to renovate my kitchen but don't know where to start. Can you help me?

For sure! I've designed kitchens in the past and have the experience, know-how and connections in order to help make this a smooth and efficiently process. Send me a message about it and let's talk!

I need some shelves built in my closet, is this something you can do?

absolutely! I excel at making spaces more efficient and functional for everyday usage.
