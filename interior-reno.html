Cabinetry & Built-ins

Custom cabinetry and built-ins tell a story of style, function, and the warmth of a handcrafted touch. By putting care first, I craft custom carpentry that will fit your needs and your space just right.

Built-in qualities

Care

Pride in my work goes hand in hand with a dedication to care. Regardless of budget constraints, compromising on quality is a line I never cross. Each project benefits from hand-selected materials, and the application of time-tested craftsmanship. I aspire to create pieces that carry an element of artistry, destined to become cherished objects in your home, offering enjoyment for years to come.

Intention

Crafting a space to reflect an individual’s unique personality or preferences is exceptionally rewarding. Customization not only adds a personal touch but also maximizes the efficiency of space management. When I delve into creating custom built-in storage and cabinets, I unlock the opportunity to design spaces that align perfectly with the homeowner’s lifestyle. It’s not just about fitting things in; it’s about crafting a space that works seamlessly and intentionally for them. I feel honoured to contribute to this personalized approach.

Respect

My journey in crafting custom cabinets and built-ins began in the realm of challenging projects with distinctive layouts. These were spaces where off-the-shelf cabinets or store-bought models simply wouldn’t suffice, or if used, would require substantial modifications. In other words, my experience sprouted from necessity rather than luxury, fostering a profound respect for all forms of custom cabinetry. It’s this respect that fuels my dedication to creating solutions that fit perfectly while elevating the functionality and aesthetics of every space.

FAQs

Here are some of the questions I am most commonly asked about building cabinetry and built-ins.

View all FAQs

Can you build simple open shelving for my closet and storage area?

Yes, I’d be more than happy to help you with that. Open shelving is a fantastic choice for closets and storage areas, offering both practicality and a touch of style. I take great pride in focusing on the details that matter to you, so that your specific needs and functionality are at the forefront of any design.

How do you handle extras or overruns?

When an unforeseen detail or specific added request comes up, I provide an additional quote for approval or work on a verbal time-plus-material agreement with documented expenses and labour. However, my exact approach depends on the amount of additional work required and the relationship with the client.

Do you provide detailed estimates for approval?

Yes. My quotes are broken down into labour and materials and often provide additional details in an accompanying email. This may include suggested timeliness, material options or details, maintenance requirements, and other information specific to the project.

Can you trim out my entire house or do you just do smaller jobs?

I have extensive experience in installing full house trim packages. Big or small, I can help!
