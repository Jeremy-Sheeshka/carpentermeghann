Decks, Fences, Gates & Railings

A unique wooden fence, deck, or gate is a guaranteed way to enhance the enjoyment and security of your outdoor space. With the skills of a professional carpenter, you can achieve the ideal blend of style and functionality to contemporize any outdoor living space.

Lets design a fence that defines your outdoor space.

  • Wooden Privacy & Chainlink Fences

    Wood is a timeless and adaptable material that can be shaped to fit many different looks and designs.

    From overlapping tongue and groove fences, to the classic picket fence, I can build any fence to accomodate the vision you might have within your space. Be it recycled wood, driftwood, or repurposed wooden siding, I can make it happen.

  • Lattice & Wooden Screens

    Decorative wooden fences such as lattice can infuse both elegance and style to your outdoor space.

    Be it a support structure for greenery, or to help delineate sections in a garden, lattice is an aesthetic intricate pattern that can be a contemporary choice for your space. I can build to suit many different styles (horizontal & vertical, interlocking diagnoal, etc.) many different designs to best fit both your vision and be suitable for your existing landscape.

    Whether it's standing alone or combined with wood, steel or other elements, if you are seeking something that endures the elements and is adaptable to many landescapes, a solid concrete wall might be for you be it a freestanding or retaining wall.

FAQs

Here are some of the questions I am most commonly asked about my decks, fences & gates.

How do you manage additional charges or unforeseen circumstances on your billing?

When an unexpected detail or a specific additional request arises, I either provide a separate quote for approval or proceed with a verbal time-and-materials agreement, keeping a record of expenses and labor. The exact approach depends on the extent of the additional work required and the nature of my relationship with the client.

Can you also paint or stain the fence or gate you build for me?

Definitely! Painting or staining lumber before or after it s constructed can enhance the beauty of wood and improve the lifespan of the structure.

Do your fences come with any type of warranty; are they built to withstand the elements of Prince Rupert?

With my careful planning and attention to detail to the structures I create, I stand by and am proud of the quality of my work. I always do what I can to make sure I produce is of superior quality and will reasonably stand the test of time.

Are you able to fix, paint, or restain my existing deck, fence or gate?

When it comes to remediating an existing deck, fence or gate, I like to think of myself as a bit of an expert. Having worked on various historical restoration projects in the past, I know I can find the right solution to fix your existing deck or fence.
