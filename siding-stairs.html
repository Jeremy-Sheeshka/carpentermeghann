My guiding values

If you can dream it, I can build it. You bet. But what I believe distinguishes me from others in this industry is how I approach each project through my core values.

Care

Care is at the heart of everything I do, and goes beyond simply completing a task. I bring a deep sense of responsibility, empathy, and attention to detail to my work. I invest time, effort, and genuine concern, treating the client and the project itself with utmost respect and consideration.

Sincerity

Sincerity plays a significant role in shaping my values. Transparency and open, honest communication. Clear timelines, costs, and challenges. Ethical standards and respect for all ideas. And of course, pride in delivering on my promises.

Craft

My approach goes beyond conforming to industry norms. I strive to break free from the “splash and dash” model that is often seen in modern contracting. I can work with most budgets, but I don’t take shortcuts or compromise.

Observation

I have a keen eye for “reading” the environment I am working in. I can quickly assess the situation, identify potential issues, and come up with creative solutions. This allows me to create pieces that harmonize with the surroundings and enhance the overall ambience.

Connection

Fostering a strong client relationship plays a huge role in my approach to carpentry. I prioritize open communication, and take the time to listen actively. I strive to understand my client’s vision, and genuinely care about their satisfaction with the finished product.

Standards

I understand the value and beauty of wood and other materials. Where possible, I carefully select high-quality materials that will not only stand the test of time but also enhance the overall aesthetics of the project. Every piece of wood tells a different story.

Mindfulness

I approach my craft with presence and attention, giving my undivided attention to the make-or-break details and paying close attention to every cut, join, and finish. Beyond the visuals, a mindful approach also leads to structurally sound, long-lasting work.

Intention

I view each project with a clear vision and purpose, considering the functionality, aesthetics, and client’s preferences while designing. With intention, my work can convey a sense of purpose, mindfulness, and respect for both the process and the final result.
