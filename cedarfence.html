Windows & Doors

Windows & Doors of your house often form your first impression of a structure. With a custom door, the entrance to your home and property can be both a uniquely warm welcome to guests, and a protective barrier for your family.

How would you like your home to say hello?

  • Custom Exterior Doors

    A unique exterior door can transform an entryway into something truly extraordinary.

    An eye catching exterior door plays a pivotal role in first impressions of a house that can reflect a quality of lifesyle, design preference, or highlight the unique character of your home.

  • Interior Doors & Entryways

    A fine tuned interior door can not only provide a seamless transition between personal spaces but also contribute to an appealing atmosphere of a household.

    Whether you are after a contemporary look, desire more sound deadening qualities of a solid core door, or just want someone to make an existing door close a little more easily, I would be honored to help.

  • Swinging/Sliding Gates & Doors

    A well hung gate or door should not only swing, slide, or close with ease but be finely attuned to its' surroundings.

    Whether decorative or practical in nature, a swinging or sliding gate or door adds a touch of drama to the space it's in through its movement. Regardless of whether it's on a fence, shed, garage, or an interior track, these doors can be an essential element of a house's narrative.

  • Prefabricated & Custom Windows

    Residential windows have the ability to elevate a room or a space by letting in natural light and beauty in from the outside.

    When installed correctly, a prefabricated window can be both a functional and elegant upgrade for your house that influences the overall feel of your home while lasting a lifetime.

FAQs

Here are some of the questions I am most commonly asked about windows and doors.

My door no longer shuts properly! Can you help me fix it?

Challenge Accepted! Be it through planing the door or adjusting the hardware, I've got many tricks up my sleeve to get your door shutting properly again.

I have a single pane window in my house I want to upgrade. Can you help find and install a suitable replacement?

Yes ofcourse. I love taking out windows and am happy to work with your existing window's opening to find the perfect replacement.

Are you able to make custom interior window casings and trim?

For sure! While I love particularly love working with cedar, I also have a fondness of reusing and upcycling materials you might already have around. I would be happy to accomodate whatever materials, preferences, or budget you like and the desired look you wish.

I need a new exterior door! Is this something you can help me with?

Absolutely! Based on the condition of the frame, I can determine for if it's possible to swap out just the door slab, or better to install a new pre-hung unit.
