FAQs

Here are some of the questions I am most commonly asked about my woodworking services in general.

View all FAQs

Can you build simple open shelving for my closet and storage area?

Yes, I’d be more than happy to help you with that. Open shelving is a fantastic choice for closets and storage areas, offering both practicality and a touch of style. I take great pride in focusing on the details that matter to you, so that your specific needs and functionality are at the forefront of any design.

Can you help with the design of my fence or privacy screen?

Yes, Donnelly Woodworking can work with you to design a custom fence or privacy screen that meets your needs and complements the style of your home. I can provide guidance on materials, layout, and other design elements to ensure that your fence or privacy screen is both functional and beautiful.

Can you install prefab or flat-pack cabinets?

Certainly, I have the capability to install prefabricated or flat-pack cabinets and ensure they are set up properly in your space. However, it’s worth noting that these types of cabinets may sometimes fall short in terms of durability, fit, and unique design characteristics. Custom work allows for higher quality materials, personalized design, and significantly increased longevity.

What is your service area?

Donnelly Woodworking offers service to central Vancouver Island and the Gulf Islands. I am also willing to travel when a unique project really sparks some interest outside of this area.
