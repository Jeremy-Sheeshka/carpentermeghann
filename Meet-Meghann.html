Meet Meghann

I’m the owner, the carpenter, the bookkeeper and the project manager; a Red Seal Carpenter and a woodworker with over twenty years of industry experience. My name is Meghann Barsalou and I’m pleased to meet you!

Functional design.
Artisan quality.
Heartfelt care.

My approach to carpentry and work ethic is steered by my inner values, which can be seen in every my every endeavour

These values are what I believe transcend my work beyond just constructing or designing functional and aesthetic projects, but ensure that the end product which is crafted is done so with mindfulness and attention to detail.

Professional skills?
You name it, I can do it.

I'm a Red Seal Carpenter with 20+ years working in various residential, commercial and large-industry environments. I offer my clients an extensive skill set, a plethora of real world experiences, and the tools to efficiently accomplish any job big or small. As a woman carpenter, I take pride in drawing upon my unique network and connections with local tradespeople and organizations in Prince Rupert and beyond, and am confident I can accomplish any job big or small.

Through my imagination to reality approach in residential construction, I am able to offer my experiences in both custom and contemporary design as well as historical restoration and traditional styles. .


Beyond the skills,
my work is about care and connection.

When I'm working for you, we're family. This is one factor that I believe sets me apart from others; it's not just the professional skills I can offer, but it's about the way in which I am able to put implement them into my work.

From the first draft to the final design phase to the fine-tuning, I take pride in my work.

For me, carpentry goes beyond merely completing a task; it's about creating something that brings happiness to the world and showcases my unwavering commitment to exceptional craftsmanship.
