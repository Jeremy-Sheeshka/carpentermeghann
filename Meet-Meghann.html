Meet Meghann

I’m the owner, the carpenter, the bookkeeper and the project manager; a Red Seal Carpenter and a woodworker with over twenty years of industry experience. My name is Meghann Barsalou, and and I’m pleased to meet you!

My approach

I suppose I should tell you what I excel at.

Perhaps that I specialize in thinking outside the box. That I strive to craft seamless transitions and achieve a soothing visual balance in every project.

That exceptional fit and finish are paramount to me, and that every detail is executed with precision.

And how I take pride in my design and execution, and don't believe in taking shortcuts.

But the most important thing I want to share?

I care.

Functional design.
Artisan quality.
Heartfelt care.

I cannot express enough that I honestly care for the product I produce.

Intention, mindfulness, and care are values ingrained in my work ethic, and reflected in the final outcome of the projects I undertake.

It is not just about creating a functional and aesthetically-pleasing object, but also ensuring that it is crafted with intention and attention to detail.

👉 The Thoughts section of this site includes a variety of articles that take a deeper look into the values that govern my work.

Technical skills?
You bet!

I am trained in modern methods (I'm a Red Seal carpenter with 20+ years on the job), but also have an extensive skill set.

Through my form-to-finish experience in custom residential construction, I am able to achieve both a contemporary and a traditional style.

But trust me when I say, with the upmost respect for the technical skill involved, that anyone can achieve these skills.

Really though,
it's all about care

What sets me apart is not just the technical skills I possess, but the manner in which I execute them.

Again I come back to the care and sincerity that I infuse into my work. Mindfulness. Intention. From the initial design phase to the final touches, I choose to deliver these values in every project I take on.

Because it's not merely about completing a job, but about creating something that brings joy into the world and stands as a testament to my commitment to quality craftsmanship.

  • I can work with all manner of budgets

  • I can build in a wide range of styles

  • I can collaborate closely or take the lead
